Do I need to register to use Railway ?

You do not need to register when exploring our trips, directions, prices and the possibility of booking any of the selected trips, but you need to register when you make a reservation on any of the trips.

I entered the wrong phone number while booking. Can I get a ticket on another number?

Once the reservation is made, the mobile and the customer's name is registered with this reservation and cannot be modified, but it is possible to ride on the appointment in name only - and the phone number must be changed in order to be registered correctly with the next reservation.

What should I do if I lost my ticket?

Go to the station, and a receipt is made to the customer after reviewing the info on the site at an amount of 25 pounds for each ticket.

Is it mandatory to take a hard copy of the ticket?

There is no need to get a printed copy of the ticket when paying with a credit card system as the reservation data is recorded so you can be present directly to your. In case of requesting home delivery, our representative will receive the printed tickets and you must keep them and take them on the flight.

Can I switch to another train?

you can't switch to another train once 24 hours have passed since booking time.

Does the child need a seat?

For any child 4 years of age and older, they must purchase a seat ticket.
